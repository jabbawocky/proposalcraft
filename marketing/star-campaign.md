# GitHub Star Ask — Pre-Launch Campaign

**When to send:** Evening of June 9 (night before PH launch).

**Why this matters:** Product Hunt surfaces GitHub star count. Directories (Glama, mcp.so) rank by stars. 20+ stars before launch creates social proof that prevents the "0 stars" cold-start problem — people hesitate to be the first adopter.

**Who to ask:** Developer friends, ex-colleagues, anyone who'd spend 10 seconds for you. You need ~20 people who'll actually do it. Don't send to everyone — 5 people who star is better than 50 people who open and close the tab.

**Time required:** 10 minutes to send the messages. 10 seconds per person to actually star.

---

## Message variants (copy-paste)

### Text / WhatsApp (10 seconds for them)

> Launching my side project on Product Hunt tomorrow — would massively help if you starred the GitHub repo before midnight. Literally takes 10 seconds: https://github.com/jabbawocky/proposalcraft — tap ⭐ in the top right. Thanks legend.

## Who to target (ranked)

1. **Developer friends** — most likely to have GitHub accounts and actually do it
2. **Ex-colleagues in tech** — same
3. **Freelancers you know** — might not have GitHub, but many do. Worth asking.
4. **LinkedIn connections in dev/consulting** — lower conversion, worth a shot for a few key people

**Avoid:** People who don't have GitHub accounts (won't bother creating one). People you don't know well (feels spammy). Mass blasting your whole contact list (gets ignored).

---

## Day-of follow-up (optional)

After the PH post is live, send a shorter message to the same people:

> [Link to PH post] — just launched! If you have a sec, an upvote on PH helps too. Thanks again for the star 🙏

Don't ask for both in the first message — one ask at a time.

PH voting opens at midnight PST. Get the star campaign done by 11pm PST Jun 9 so it counts before launch.
