# Personal Outreach Email — Launch Day

Send this to people you actually know: past clients, colleagues who freelance, friends who consult. This is your highest-conversion channel — people who trust you are 10x more likely to try something you recommend than strangers reading a Reddit post.

**When to send:** Morning of June 10 (or evening of June 9 for people in earlier time zones).

**Who to send it to:**
- Past freelance/consulting clients (especially ones who commission proposals from others)
- Colleagues who do consulting or freelance work on the side
- Anyone who's complained to you about proposal writing
- Anyone in your network who works with agencies, freelancers, or contractors

---

## Subject line options (pick one)

- "Shipped something that saves me 2 hours per proposal"
- "Built a thing I think you'll actually use"
- "Quick one — something I made for freelancers"

## Email body

Hi [Name],

And if you try it: let me know what you think. The honest feedback is what I actually want right now.

## Tips

- **BCC everyone, or send individually** — personalise where you can, even one sentence ("I know you do a lot of agency pitches…")
- **No need to explain MCP or Claude in detail** — the landing page does that. Your job is to get them to click.
- **Follow up in 3 days** with anyone who clicks but doesn't reply — one short "did you get a chance to try it?" is fine.
- **LinkedIn DM version:** Same content, even shorter. Just the hook and the link.
