## Pitch principles

- Lead with their article, not your product — credibility first
- One sentence on what it does: "Drafts proposals in your voice from past wins"
- Remove objections upfront: free, MIT, no API key, one-line install
- Offer to write the blurb — makes their yes frictionless
- Don't pitch as "the best" — pitch as filling a gap they're missing

A single inclusion in a top-10 article = estimated 50–200 installs/month passively.
