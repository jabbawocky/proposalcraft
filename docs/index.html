Hi Jamie,

Thanks for reaching out — a conversion-focused redesign on an established Shopify store is exactly the kind of project I enjoy. A few things in your brief stood out: the drop in conversion rate is almost always a combination of trust signals, mobile friction, and checkout abandonment, and all three are fixable.

Here's how I'd approach it:

Discovery & Audit (Week 1–2)
Review your current analytics to identify the exact drop-off points, audit the 10 highest-traffic pages, and map a revised site architecture.

Design (Weeks 3–5)
Mobile-first redesign of Homepage, Collection pages, Product pages, and Checkout — delivered in Figma with 2 rounds of revisions.

Build on Shopify (Weeks 6–10)
Custom theme development within your existing Shopify plan...

How it works

Three steps. That's it.

Install ProposalCraft

Add one JSON block to your Claude Desktop config. Restart Claude.

Save a winning proposal

Paste one of your past proposals. ProposalCraft stores it and learns your style.

Paste the client brief

Ask Claude to draft a proposal. It uses your voice, their brief. Send it.
