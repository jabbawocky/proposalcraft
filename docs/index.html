How it works

Three steps. That's it.

Install ProposalCraft

Add one JSON block to your Claude Desktop config. Restart Claude.

Save a winning proposal

Paste one of your past proposals. ProposalCraft stores it and learns your style.

Paste the client brief

Ask Claude to draft a proposal. It uses your voice, their brief. Send it.
